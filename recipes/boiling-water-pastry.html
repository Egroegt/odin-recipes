Boiling Water Pastry

No Fail Pastry

    Shopping List
  • 1 cup shortening
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 2 cups cake flour
  • 1 pinch salt
      • Instructions
  • Place shortening into a heat-proof bowl. Pour boiling water and whip mixture until creamy.
  • Mix together flour and salt in a separate bowl. Add flour mixture to shortening mixture.
  • Stir until pastry stops sticking to the sides of the bowl. Refrigerate pastry before rolling it out
